Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Important: This Privacy Policy describes how Numora collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our document management application.

1. Introduction

At Numora, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address when you create an account
  • Document Data: Content of receipts and financial documents you upload to organize and process
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you use our application to improve our services
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system information for compatibility

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our document management services
  • Process and organize your documents using text recognition technology
  • Communicate with you about our service when necessary
  • Protect against unauthorized access to your account
  • Improve the user experience of our application

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us provide our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Control how we use your information
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in our application and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
